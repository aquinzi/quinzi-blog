No no, I'm not going to post in japanese. I haven't reached that level yet :P .

Few days ago I was thinking in different ways of making my poor japanese vocab bigger. Between many options, I decided to read texts... all japanese texts. What do I mean by that? well, reading manga!

Why manga and not something else? The reason why is because of a) it has drawings, which helps for the understanding whatever the sentientbeings are saying. b) short "phrases" (which I don't know if it's good or bad). c) Informal language, which is very... difficult. d)... e) it has drawings.

I got the manga I wanted, cbzed them and sent them to the kindle. First try failed. Furigana is hard to see already, cannot even see them on the kindle. Kanji is hard to see already, cannot even see them on the kindle. If I had an advanced level it wouldn't be a problem, but I'm still a beginner. That's why I decided to keep the translations on the kindle for guidance. Besides that, it's a pain in the butt translate words or look for grammatical explanations on the cellphone.

Second try: what I'm doing now and it's not so troublesome. Read cbz on the pc. YEAH! Zipped images reader + browser + note taking program + paper = win. Now I have to use a second monitor so it's more comfortable :) .

You might be asking how it went, right? If you didn't stick your mouse in your eyes.Good... good... really... well, no SUPER WOW GOOD but it wasn't that bad.

The 5 or so pages were a mini chaos. Not knowing "everyday" vocabulary + informal language + regionalism is... horrible T-T. After, I got the hang out of it. Then, I got sick of looking words up in the dictionary that I decided to get the translations for a bit of guidance. Minutes later, I didn't even care (or wanted to finish quickly) that I just read, no matter if I understood or not.

You all will say, why not letting this for later? 1) don't wanna. 2) in some sort of way is fun discovering words. 3) the feeling of "I know thiiiiis onneeeeee and didn't need a dictionary! weeeeeeeeeeeeeee" 4) etc. And started to feel like this:

How cute ^_^

So yeah. I've got tons of manga to read, like 45+ O_O I must learn something from it XD
